How to use the exports

Daily Attendance Log (CSV) — columns Date, Present, Absent, Leave, OT_Hours. Append/paste these rows into HR_Attendance_Daily_Log_June2026.csv (or the linked "HR ATTENDANCE" Google Sheet) to feed the VC Dashboard's HR & Attendance module.

Monthly Present Days & OT (CSV) — columns Emp Code, Employee Name, Present Days, OT Hours. Use this to update the "Present Days" (col I) and "OT Hours" (col J) of the relevant month sheet (Apr-Mar) in SALARY_MASTER_SHEET.xlsx — Gross Salary, Salary Payable, Advance Master, Yearly Summary and Dashboard recalculate automatically from those two columns.

Flagged Records Report (CSV) — full audit trail of every punch anomaly and how it was corrected, for HR sign-off.
