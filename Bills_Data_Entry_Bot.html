📋 Inventory Master (Item List & Opening Stock)

Upload your Inventory Master (Excel: columns Item Code, Item Name, Unit, Opening Stock, Reorder Level) once. Saved Sales/Purchase bills will then update Closing Stock automatically and persist on this device (browser storage) — no need to re-upload each time.

📦 Inventory Impact (Opening → Closing Stock)

Purchase bill items = stock IN (+) · Sales bill items = stock OUT (−). Expense bills do not affect inventory. Closing Stock = Opening Stock + Total IN − Total OUT, recalculated automatically every time a Sales/Purchase bill is saved. Items found in bills that aren't in your Inventory Master show Opening Stock = 0 — add them to the master for accurate balances.

⬇ Export Registers

How to use the exports

Sales / Purchase / Expense Register (CSV) — one row per bill, ready to append to your Accounts / Sales / Purchase logs feeding the VC Dashboard's Accounts, Sales and Purchase modules.

Inventory Movement (CSV) — per-item IN/OUT quantities from every bill processed this session.

Updated Inventory Master (Excel) — your Inventory Master with stock levels rolled forward (Opening + IN − OUT = new Opening), ready to save as your next Inventory Master file — this is the "automatic inventory update" output.

All data — registers, inventory log and item master — is saved automatically in this browser (local storage) so it stays available next time you open this file on this device.

All data is processed locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.
