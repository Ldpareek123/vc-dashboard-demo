One-time setup (Gmail): create a label called SSPI-Sales-Orders in Gmail and apply it to incoming customer order emails (manually, or with a Gmail filter rule). Every 30 minutes the system scans that label. If the email has a PDF/XLSX/DOCX attachment, it's saved to Drive and linked below. If the email has no attachment — e.g. the customer pasted an order/projection table straight into the email body — that table is captured automatically too; click 📄 View Captured Content on that row to see it and pull any line into a real order. Either way, the order amount/product is left blank for you to complete here.
